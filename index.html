This is an image of Kratos from God of War exclusively on PS4. This is the eighth installment in the series, but the first of the series to venture outside of the realm of Greek mythology and into the realm of Norse mythology. This is one of my favorite video game franchises. The way the developers and the producers tweak small things about the mythos in each game, to make Kratos feel like he was actually a part of it is one of my favorite things about the franchise. I wouldn't be mad if the battle between Kratos and Aries was written in some mythology books in the future. Hehe

A few facts/information about God of War on PS4, and Norse Mythology:

  • Cory Balrog was the the mastermind behind the masterpiece
  • Kratos' son, you learn to be the Norse god of mischief, Loki
  • Loki is actually the brother of Odin, unlike Loki depicted in the marvel universe. Very common misconception of Norse Mythology.
